The types of information we collect

Information you give us:

Some iBox Printers devices and applications allow automatic or manual input of data, such as model, license, and creator name. This data is sent to third parties for the purpose of soliciting information on the product associated with the models created and/or shared on iBox servers and systems.

Information required to access third party systems:

Some iBox Printers devices and applications allow connectivity to third party applications / websites / servers. Some of these require you to enter a user name and password, where in some cases the user name could be an email address. All user information entered to access a specific third party is sent only to the third party in which you are intending to connect. The data is never stored on any iBox Printers Servers or transferred off of the device to any other partner or sold or distributed in any way. iBox Printers has no access to this data as it is stored on the local device.

¥   For example if you send a post to Facebook from a iBox Printers application, you will need to log on to Facebook in order to gain access. This logon information is only sent to Facebook. This information is not sent to any other party. This information is not collected by iBox Printers.

Information we receive from third parties

iBox Printers receives sales information from third parties when a purchase is made from an Affiliate of iBox Printers. This sales information contains no personally identifiable information. This information can include the product purchased, quantity, date/time, retailer, price and fees. It will not include personal information that may or may not have been provided to the third part during the purchase.

¥   For Example; if you are using a iBox Printers application to order consumables that is fulfilled by a third party, then the minimum information to fulfill the transaction will be shared with the third party.

iBox Printers is not responsible for, and will assume no liability, if a business partner or other entity collects, uses or shares any information about you in violation of its own privacy policy or applicable law.

Information that you enter in an in-app or on-device web browser

Information entered in any iBox Printers on-device or in-app web browser is not shared or sold by iBox Printers. iBox Printers will store your email address, logon information including password on the local device and on our servers. This data will not be shared or sold.

Automatic Data Collection

Tracking technology: These technologies collect non-personally identifiable information to improve or monitor iBox Printers devices and applications, including to keep count of return visits to our devices and applications, website or our advertisers' or partners' websites, collect and report on aggregate statistical information.  We may also capture other data such as search criteria and results, date, time, connection speed and IP addresses.

Third parties whose products or services are accessible or advertised via a iBox Printers application may use cookies, and we advise you to check their privacy policies for information about their cookies and other privacy practices.

 

How the information we collect is shared:

We will make your personally identifiable information available to other companies, websites, applications or people in the circumstances listed below.

¥   You provide information in the course of your use of the Service by participating in community and social networking features such as Facebook.

¥   When we hire or partner with third parties to provide specialized services on our behalf, such as coupon services and special products or services that we choose to make available to our users.

¥   In connection with a merger with another entity, or in the event of a transfer of all or some of our assets.

¥   When we have a good faith belief that we are required to do so by law or in response to a law enforcement agency's request, or in a situation where disclosing your personal information is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be causing injury to iBox Printers, our users or members of the public.
